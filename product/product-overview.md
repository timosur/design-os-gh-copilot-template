# TeamVibe

## Description
TeamVibe is a lightweight mood check-in tool for small teams that captures how everyone is feeling at the start of a meeting using a simple emoji scale. A facilitator kicks off a session, the team votes anonymously, and everyone instantly sees an aggregate summary — helping teams surface unspoken tension, celebrate good energy, and start every meeting with intention.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Meetings Start Without Emotional Context
Team members often jump straight into agendas without knowing how everyone is feeling. Someone might be stressed, overwhelmed, or disengaged — and nobody notices until it's too late.
TeamVibe gives every meeting a 30-second emotional pulse check so facilitators can adapt the tone, pace, or agenda based on how the room actually feels.

### Problem 2: People Don't Speak Up About How They Feel
In group settings, it's uncomfortable to say "I'm not doing great today." This leads to hidden frustration and disengagement that compounds over time.
TeamVibe uses anonymous emoji voting so people can be honest without feeling exposed. The group sees only the aggregate — no names attached.

### Problem 3: Facilitators Lack a Quick, Low-Friction Tool
Existing tools are either too heavyweight (full survey platforms) or too informal (asking "how's everyone doing?" and getting silence). There's no purpose-built, fast check-in tool.
TeamVibe is designed for exactly one thing: a fast, focused mood check-in. Start a session, share a code, see results — done in under a minute.

### Problem 4: No Shared Awareness of Team Energy
Without a visible signal, teams can't collectively acknowledge their state. High-energy moments go uncelebrated; low-energy moments go unaddressed.
TeamVibe displays a live group summary (emoji distribution, average mood) so the whole team has a shared picture of how they're doing right now.

## Key Features
- Facilitator-initiated sessions with shareable join codes
- Emoji-based mood scale for quick, intuitive voting
- Anonymous responses — no individual attribution
- Live aggregate results displayed as charts and averages
- Simple join flow — no account required for participants
- Mobile-friendly design for on-the-go check-ins
